# Monetra — Google Play Store Metadata Specification

## Store Listing Metadata

- **App Name**: Monetra — Privacy Finance Vault
- **Short Description**: Production-grade, offline-first personal finance, budgeting, and privacy analytics.
- **Full Description**:
Monetra is a production-ready, privacy-first personal finance platform designed to give you complete control over your money without sacrificing your security.

Key Features:
- 100% Offline Vault: Zero cloud sync, zero telemetry, zero analytics.
- Accounts & Transactions Studio: Manage checking, savings, cash, and investment balances with multi-currency support.
- Flexible Budgets Engine: Category spending limits with real-time rollover progress indicators.
- Goals & Savings Planner: Savings targets, debt payoff calculators, and progress forecasts.
- Custom Reports & Export Engine: Export financial summaries to CSV, structured JSON, or GitHub Flavored Markdown (GFM).
- Containerized Backup Archives (.monetra): Encrypted, versioned local database archives.
- Security & Privacy Studio: PIN lock keypad, biometric unlock, and Privacy Mode amount masking.
- Universal Command Palette (Ctrl+K): Instant search across all accounts, categories, transactions, and commands.
